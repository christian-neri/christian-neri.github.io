Home · Work · Studiocanal

A flagship web presence for one of the world's largest film libraries — designed to respect legacy IP while signaling future-forward distribution strategy.

Scope

IA, UX, art direction

Challenge

Minimal footprint, premium read

Goal

Elevate corporate storytelling

Context

Studiocanal's corporate site must surface complex library offerings, global partnerships, and investor-grade clarity without overwhelming visitors. The mandate: deliver an editorially confident experience that reads premium even when page templates stay intentionally lean.

Goal

Information architecture, art direction, and UX to elevate a minimal footprint into a premium corporate showcase. The redesign sequences proof points cinematically — bold hero statements, tactile modules for featured slates, and structured pathways for institutional audiences.

Approach

Hierarchies were stress-tested across stakeholders spanning marketing, acquisitions, and press relations. Component families balance modular scalability with cinematic photography treatments, emphasizing texture and depth over ornament. Interaction patterns prioritize scan-friendly hierarchy and frictionless localization hooks for global teams.
